Crispy and Tender Chicken Katsu

Japanese style fried chicken. Can also be used to make Tonkatsu, just use pork cutlets instead of chicken.

Serve with white rice and tonkatsu sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves - pounded to 2cm thickness
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp all-purpose flour
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 130g panko bread crumbs
  • oil for frying

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Season the chicken breasts on both sides with salt and pepper. Place the flour, egg and panko crumbs into separate shallow dishes.

    Coat the chicken breasts in flour, shaking off any excess.

    Dip them into the egg, and then press into the panko crumbs until well coated on both sides.

  2. Step 2

    3. Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

    Place chicken in the hot oil, and cook 3 or 4 minutes per side, or until golden brown.

    Set aside.

    Enjoy!
