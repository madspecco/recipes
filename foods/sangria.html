Tasty Sangria for Summer Parties

The best sangria recipe out there for summer parties!

It is strongly recommended to use a fruity Spanish red wine, such as Garnacha.

Ingredients

  • 1 bottle dry red wine
  • 100ml brandy
  • 100ml triple sec
  • 70ml lemonade concentrate
  • 70ml orange juice
  • 50ml lemon juice
  • 50g white sugar(although optional)
  • 1 medium orange, sliced
  • 1 medium lemon, sliced
  • 1 medium lime, sliced
  • 8 maraschino cherries
  • 200ml carbonated water(optional)

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Mix together red wine, brandy, triple sec, lemonade concentrate, orange juice, lemon juice, and sugar in a bowl.

    Add orange, lemon, and lime slices and maraschino cherries.

  2. Step 2

    3. Serve immediately over ice, or refrigerate 8 hours to overnight for best flavor.

  3. Step 3

    4. For a fizzy sangria, add club soda just before serving.

    Enjoy!
