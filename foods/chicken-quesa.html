Delicious Chicken Quesadillas

This chicken quesadilla recipe is great to make for parties. Zesty chicken, cooked peppers, and melted cheese are a delightful combination.

Cut into wedges and serve with sour cream and salsa.

Ingredients

  • 150g skinless, boneless chicken breast, diced
  • fajita seasoning
  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 green bell peppers, chopped
  • 2 red bell peppers, chopped
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 10 flour tortillas
  • 200g shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 tbsp bacon bits

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the broiler. Grease a baking sheet.

  2. Step 2

    3. Toss chicken with fajita seasoning, then spread onto the baking sheet.

    Place under the broiler and cook until chicken is no longer pink in the center, about 5 minutes.

  3. Step 3

    4. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  4. Step 4

    5. Heat oil in a large saucepan over medium heat. Stir in bell peppers, onion, and broiled chicken.

    Cook and stir until vegetables have softened, about 10 minutes.

  5. Step 5

    6. Layer half of each tortilla with chicken and vegetable mixture, Cheddar cheese, Monterey Jack cheese, and bacon bits.

    Fold tortillas in half and place onto a baking sheet.

  6. Step 6

    7. Bake quesadillas in the preheated oven until cheeses have melted, about 10 minutes.

  7. Step 7

    8. Cut each quesadilla into 4 wedges and serve.

    Enjoy!
